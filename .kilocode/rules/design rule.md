# design rule.md

-These are the official Afroheat design rules. Follow them exactly across all components, modes, and layouts.

## Guidelines
Logos
-Always use the Primary Logo on light and white backgrounds.
-Use Purple Logo only on light/white backgrounds when needed.
-Use Inverted Logo on purple or dark backgrounds.
-Use Black Logo only if color is not possible.
-Use White on Black Logo on black backgrounds if color is not possible.
-Never reproduce the logo smaller than 17 mm (print) or 50 px (screen).
-For favicons/icons, use the icon only (no text).
-Logo and text must always be locked together with “AFROHEAT” in uppercase.
-Maintain clearspace equal to the width of the letter A around the logo.
-Place logo in the center of the artboard whenever possible.
-Logo eyeballs must always appear brighter than the background.
-Do not distort, rotate, skew, add effects, or modify the logo.
-Do not use off-brand colors, low-contrast backgrounds, or unofficial graphics.

Colors
-Primary Color: #8c1e81
-Secondary Color: #fab313
-Accent Colors: #f1e5c4, #B0D88E, #276E71, #e40b65
-Use colors in ratio: 60% primary, 30% secondary, 10% accents.
-Colors must always reflect the brand mood: Energetic, Bold, African, Strong.

Typography

-Primary Font: Poppins (Light, Medium, Bold).
-Title Font: Industry Black, BLOCK BOLD UPPERCASE, only for large headlines.
-Amharic Font: Noto Sans Ethiopic Bold for all Amharic text.
-Maintain font hierarchy and consistency across all elements.
-Do not substitute with off-brand fonts.

Photography
-Use images that are: Exciting, Bold, Colorful, Warm, Active.
-Avoid dull, muted, or low-energy visuals.
-Social Media / Applications
-Content must always be: Creative, Flowing, Fun, Entertaining, Bold, Professional.
-Apply brand logos, typography, and colors consistently.

General Rules
-Preserve brand integrity at all times.
-Always ensure high contrast and readability.
-Never deviate from logos, fonts, or color palettes.
-Afroheat identity must always feel Energetic, Bold, African, Strong.
